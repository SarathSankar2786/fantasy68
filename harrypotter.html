Harry Potter Movie Series

Explore the magical journey of The Boy Who Lived through all eight enchanting films.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone (2001)

Harry discovers he's a wizard and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry for the first time. He makes lifelong friends and faces the dark magic surrounding the Philosopher’s Stone.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (2002)

As mysterious attacks paralyze students, Harry must uncover the secret behind the legendary Chamber of Secrets and face a deadly creature lurking within Hogwarts.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (2004)

Harry learns that Sirius Black, an escaped convict, may be linked to his parents' deaths. Secrets unfold as time travel and ancient magic reveal surprising truths.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (2005)

Harry is unexpectedly entered into the dangerous Triwizard Tournament. As the challenges escalate, Voldemort returns in a terrifying climax that changes everything.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (2007)

With the Ministry of Magic in denial, Harry and friends form "Dumbledore’s Army" to fight back. The rise of Voldemort begins to fracture the wizarding world.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (2009)

As Voldemort’s power grows, Harry uncovers dark secrets about Tom Riddle’s past and the truth behind Horcruxes. A tragic loss rocks the school forever.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1 (2010)

Harry, Ron, and Hermione go on the run to destroy Voldemort’s Horcruxes. Danger and betrayal abound in this darker, more desperate chapter of their journey.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 2 (2011)

The epic finale of the saga. The Battle of Hogwarts rages, secrets are revealed, and Harry makes the ultimate sacrifice to end Voldemort’s reign once and for all.
